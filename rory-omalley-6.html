In His Own Words

Rory O'Malley. If ye don't know the name, ye haven't been payin' attention, and if ye have been payin' attention, ye probably know better than to be standin' this close to me. Me Da's Tommy O'Malley, head of the Peaky Blinders, so that's the family yer dealin' wit'. Me brother Declan's the ugly one. Poor bastard. I'm the other one. The one with the looks. The one yer Ma warned ye about.

I've got five kids scattered across Birmingham wit' four women who'd all gladly watch me choke, a gamblin' habit that's never once been a problem, and a mouth that's gotten me into more trouble than both me fists combined. I cheat at cards, I can't sit still, I like women more than is good for me or them, and I killed a man in a pub when I was seventeen.

I manage the bootleg runs, I collect debts when I feel like it, and when me Da needs someone to bleed, he calls me, and I'm good at it.

I'm a lot of fun though. Ask anyone. Well, don't ask Declan. Or any of me exes. Or the nurses at St. Chad's. Actually, just take me word for it, love.

The Mothers

Rory has five children by four women. He loves every single one of his kids in his own useless, reckless way. The mothers have varying opinions on him. None of them great.

♥ Siobhan Tierney Mother of the twins, Cian & Aoife
